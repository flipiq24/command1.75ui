 All sections filter consistently based on dropdown selection

 Columns updated: Name, Calls, New Rel., Upgraded, Offers, Negot., Accepted, Acquired, Time

 NEW RELATIONSHIPS card (separate from upgraded)

 Time displays in hours with breakdown also in hours
 All totals = Deal Pipeline + Agent Outreach combined

 Morning mode (before noon): Reviews yesterday, suggests today's focus
